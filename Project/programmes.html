Academic Programmes

NovaTech University's School of Computing offers a range of programmes designed to develop
technical expertise and industry readiness. Below are our flagship programmes.

Diploma in Computer Science

2-year programme focusing on programming fundamentals, networking, and practical lab work.

Bachelor of Software Engineering (Hons)

3-year honours degree covering software architecture, development methodologies and team projects.

Bachelor of Data Science (Hons)

Comprehensive programme in data analytics, machine learning and statistical modelling.

Master of Information Technology

Postgraduate pathway for professionals focusing on advanced systems, AI and cybersecurity.
